The Russell 'Russ' Vitale Catalogue

How To Rob

How To Rob is Russ' first mixtape after putting out 10 self-produced albums, and was released on August 18, 2014. Each song is named after an artist that has inspired Russ' music and has had large impacts in the hip-hop/rap industry overtime. Read more...

ZOO

Sixteen months after the release of There’s Really a Wolf, Russ' debut album, the 25-year-old rapper is coming back with ZOO. The album was announced on August 13, 2018. Read more...

Silence

Russ is an American Hip-Hop artist that has made quite a name for himself especially in the past few years. He released the album Silence in June of 2014 under the label Diemon. Read more...

Color Blind

Russ has two different eye colors, a condition known as heterochromia; his left eye is dark-brown and his right is light-brown. Read more...

Straight From Limbo

Straight from Limbo is a project by rising New Jersey Born Atlanta Raised Rapper/Emcee Russ. The project was released in 2013 and features 19 songs. Songs like the title track “Straight From Limbo” featuring Bugus are backed by incredible production and some mind blowing lyrics with a crazy flow and delivery. Along with songs like "C4" and "The One". Read more...

Vacation

Atlanta's Diemon crew member Russ released this project earlier this week. Peep it below. Atlanta is really coming up with a new crop of emcees and I really like the direction that it is headed.

Apollo 13

The DIEMON producer & emcee Russ Vitale has released his new full-length album Apollo 13 today. The album has 8 original records with features from members of the infamous Diemon crew. The Diemon (Do It Everyday Music Or Nothin) crew is really putting in work in this album.

Pink Elephant

The talented Russ is back after the release of his Pink Elephants EP with his album, Brain Dead. Pink Elephants was a great to the musicianship of Russ and it picked up fast. With reviews comparing Russ to Eminem it’s safe to say that Russ has a unique sound that really makes him stand out and Pink Elephantshowcases that sounds. Read more...

Brain Dead

"Brain Dead" catches Russ at his best with clever stories, plot twists, and lyrics that provide plenty of imagery. Brain Dead is a great listen and is sure to make you a fan of Russ if you are not already. Tune in. Read more...

There's Really A Wolf

There’s Really A Wolf is Russ' debut album after 10 years of hard work. The highly awaited album follows up his previous 11 projects and over 250 singles. As is his entire catalog, this album is strictly produced, mixed, mastered, and written by Russ himself. Read more..

The Edge

One could only imagine my excitement when I saw that Russ had randomly released his latest effort, The Edge. We'd heard talk of the album when he released his music video for "Yung Pacino" last month, but we were never given a release date, and if we did I wasn't awre of it. When I saw his reasoning for having a plain black cover I became even more excited. Would he be able to top his last effort? Could this possibly be the flawless album I know he's capable of making? Unfortunately, no. Read more...

5280

This is Russ' fourth project in eight months and his third album released this year, which is a testament to the hungry up and coming artist's persistent drive and dedication to his craft. Made up of thirteen original records, on 5280 you can hear how Russ has progressed as both a producer and emcee, especially as a producer when it comes to standout tracks like "Walk A Mile" & "Flashbacks."
